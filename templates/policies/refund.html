Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

1. Cancellation Policy

We value your time and ours. If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please do so at least 24 hours in advance.

Cancellations made more than 24 hours before the scheduled appointment time are eligible for a full refund.

2. Late Cancellations

Cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled appointment time are considered late cancellations and may not be eligible for a refund.

3. No-Show Policy

If you fail to show up for your appointment without prior notice ("No-Show"), no refund will be issued. Repeated no-shows may result in restrictions on future bookings.

4. Refund Process

Eligible refunds will be processed within 3–5 working days. The amount will be credited back to the original payment method used during booking.

5. Service Issues

Refunds for services rendered are generally not provided. However, if you are unsatisfied with your service, please let us know before leaving the salon so we can make it right.

6. Contact Us

For any cancellation or refund requests, please access your order history in the dashboard or contact us directly.
